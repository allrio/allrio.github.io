About Rozina

At Rozina Bakehouse & Coffee, we offer delicious, beautiful, and simple foods and beverages with Euro-Mediterranean flavors, using ingredients sourced as locally as possible. We feel a deep obligation to support Georgia’s local agriculture, knowing that only the best quality is being used in our unique recipes—recipes that will expand the mind on what is typically thought of as “Mediterranean Food”.

Chef Shay Lavi, known for his catering company “Let’s Eat!”, learned to cook from his grandmother, Rozina, and decided to name the restaurant in her honor. His passion for cooking is more than evident, and he is dedicated to creating food that is heart-warming and smile-inducing for all people.
